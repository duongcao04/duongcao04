1. The Problem – Challenges Businesses Face Today

In the digital era of 2025–2026, most Vietnamese businesses still struggle with their online presence:

  • Outdated websites load slowly (>3–5 seconds), causing over 50% of visitors to bounce immediately (Google stats).
  • Not mobile-friendly → losing the 70%+ of traffic coming from phones in Vietnam.
  • Poor Google rankings or no presence → total dependency on expensive Facebook/Google Ads (often 20–100 million VND/month for SMEs).
  • Vulnerable to hacks, DDoS attacks, or slow international loading → damaging trust and missing global opportunities.
  • No easy content management → teams wait for developers to update news/products → delays and extra costs.
  • Lack of analytics, chat, or basic CRM integration → no insight into where visitors come from or how they behave → missed sales opportunities.

Result: Stagnant online revenue, rising ad costs, and competitors with fast, SEO-optimized websites taking market share.
